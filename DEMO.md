# Demo

NoteSnap is deployed and publicly accessible — no installation required.

## Live Demo

**https://04-notesnap.vercel.app**

1. Visit the URL above and sign in with any Google account or email address.
2. Go to **Upload** and drop a photo of handwritten notes, a whiteboard, or a lecture slide (JPEG, PNG, HEIC, or text-based PDF). Select **Transcribe** or **Study Guide** mode and click Extract.
3. Review the extracted sections, assign a course and tags, then save.
4. From the saved note, click **Flashcards** or **Practice Questions** to generate study materials.
5. Open **Trash** to see soft-deleted notes (7-day restore window).
6. Open **Preferences** to change the theme — each account keeps its own settings independently.

Each account's notes, courses, tags, and preferences are fully isolated. To verify isolation, sign in with two different accounts and confirm they see separate data.

The backend API and interactive docs are also publicly accessible:

- Health check: https://notesnap.up.railway.app/api/health
- API docs: https://notesnap.up.railway.app/docs
